## Features Overview

### üé§ Practice Tab

The main practice screen where you interact with the AI tutor.

**How to use:**
1. Select your target language (Amharic or Oromo)
2. Long-press the microphone button to record
3. Speak naturally in your chosen language
4. Release the button when done
5. The AI will transcribe, correct, and provide feedback

**Tips:**
- Speak clearly and at a normal pace
- Practice daily to maintain your streak
- Try the structured lessons for guided practice

### üìñ Vocabulary Tab

Build your vocabulary with spaced repetition flashcards.

**Features:**
- **Browse Words**: View all available vocabulary
- **Search**: Find specific words or translations
- **Filter by Category**: Greetings, Food, Family, Travel, etc.
- **Mastery Levels**: Track your progress from "New" to "Mastered"
- **Practice**: Review words that need reinforcement

**Flashcard Practice:**
1. Tap "Practice" to start a review session
2. See the word in your target language
3. Think about the meaning
4. Tap to reveal the translation
5. Rate how well you knew it (Again, Hard, Good, Perfect)
6. The app will schedule the next review based on your performance

## Learning Tips

### For Beginners

1. **Start with Structured Lessons**: Use the "Structured Lessons" button on the Practice tab
2. **Focus on Basic Phrases**: Learn greetings and common expressions first
3. **Practice Daily**: Even 5 minutes a day helps maintain your streak
4. **Use Flashcards**: Reinforce vocabulary with spaced repetition

### For Intermediate Learners

1. **Challenge Yourself**: Try forming longer sentences
2. **Learn New Categories**: Explore different vocabulary themes
3. **Focus on Pronunciation**: Pay attention to the AI's corrections
4. **Mix Practice Methods**: Combine free practice with structured lessons

## Troubleshooting

### Microphone Issues

**Problem**: App can't record audio
**Solution**:
1. Go to Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí Ethio Tutor ‚Üí Permissions
2. Enable Microphone permission
3. Restart the app

### AI Response Errors

**Problem**: Not getting responses from AI
**Solution**:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Verify your API key is correctly configured
3. Try again in a few moments

### Audio Playback Issues

**Problem**: Can't hear the AI voice
**Solution**:
1. Check your device volume
2. Make sure Voice Feedback is enabled in Settings
3. Try adjusting Speech Speed
